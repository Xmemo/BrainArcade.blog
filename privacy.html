Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Brain Arcade ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of our website.

Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users. You can browse and play games on our site without creating an account or providing any personal data such as your name, email address, or location.

Cookies and Local Storage

Brain Arcade does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. The games embedded on this site are hosted via GitHub Pages and may operate in a sandboxed environment. They do not store personal data but may use browser local storage for game-specific functionalities, such as saving high scores or game progress. This data is stored only on your local device and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to display the pixelated fonts that give our site its retro feel. We may in the future use Google AdSense to display ads. These services are governed by Google's own privacy policy. We do not share any data from our site with these third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via our GitHub repository's issues page.
