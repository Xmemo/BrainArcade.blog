Get In Touch

Have a question, a suggestion for a classic game, or found a bug? I'd love to hear from you.

As this is an open-source project, the best way to get in touch is by opening an issue on our GitHub repository. This helps keep all feedback and discussions organized and transparent.

Please visit the Brain Arcade GitHub Issues Page to submit your thoughts.

Thank you for your interest and for helping make Brain Arcade better!
